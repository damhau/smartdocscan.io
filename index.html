FAQ

Do you store my documents?

No. By default we upload directly to your chosen cloud. Temporary processing (if enabled) is encrypted and discarded after completion.

Which clouds are supported?

OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, and iCloud Drive. More coming.

Will it work offline?

Scanning and OCR work offline. AI analysis and cloud sync require connectivity.

Is there a business plan?

Yes. Teams can share classification rules and centralize exports. Contact us.
